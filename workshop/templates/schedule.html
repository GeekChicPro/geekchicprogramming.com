GeekChic Workshops

'Get GeekChic' Programming Workshop for Women

Syllabus

The GeekChic workshop will provide a comfortable environment for women to network, learn about computer programming, and see if computer science may be a field you’re interested in pursuing. In just one weekend, we’ll introduce Python, demonstrate the command line, and start coding! This is our inaugural/test workshop, so we'll be asking for feedback along the way.

Friday 4/19, 6:30-8:30 PM: Plan on coming in to get everything installed on your computer. We'll provide some drinks and an opportunity for everyone to get to know each other.

Saturday 4/20, 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM: Instruction and guided practice as you test out your spiffy new computer skillz. We'll provide lunch, and we'd love it if you could join us for drinks and share your feedback with us after the workshop.

Why should YOU learn to program?

GeekChic Programming encourages women to transform technology by applying the skills they learn in our programming workshops and classes to explore innovative solutions to real-world problems. Over the last three years, President Obama has continued to emphasize the importance of STEM in education reform. The White House is committed to expanding STEM education and career opportunities for underrepresented groups, including women and girls, and the global job marketplace shows that these technical skills are in extremely high demand. There is a demonstrated need for women in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and math, and software is at the center of every STEM project.
