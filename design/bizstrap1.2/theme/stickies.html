Sticky Notes for Sidebars

Slap that sidebar with an attention grabbing sticky note.

Sticky Notes A bizstrap Exclusive

Need something on that page to get a special message across to your website visitors? Well, bizstrap has just the thing: Stickies! Yep, love them or hate them, you gotta respect their ability to grab people's attention. Let's face it, you see a sticky note and your eyes just gravitate to it. You're gonna read it even though you don't want to. That's just how effective these little devils are, and you know it. So go ahead, slap that page with a sticky note because you know that when you do that it's gonna get read.
